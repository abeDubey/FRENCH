THE FRENCH REVOLUTION (sick of studying this in history)

1789 - 1799

July 14, 1789 marked the beginning of the revolution when revolutionaries stormed the Bastille prison.

The Reign of Terror

From 1793-1794, thousands were executed by guillotine including King Louis XVI and Marie Antoinette.

Rise of Napoleon

The revolution paved way for Napoleon Bonaparte who would eventually crown himself Emperor.

Declaration of Rights

The revolution produced the Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen in 1789.

Revolutionary Wars

France fought against European monarchies who opposed the revolutionary ideas.
